Hi there :-)

My name is Fang, and I'm a CS major, mobile developer, and urban dancer from UC Berkeley.

I don't have many interests, but I'm intensely passionate about the few things I am interested in. And so, I spend most of my time split between dancing and building stuff on my mac.

Indeed, the whole reason I got into CS in the first place was because I wanted to build stuff, and so far, I'm loving every second of it.

Uniting all my interests, then, is this idea of agency, this theme of turning something deeply personal and deeply original into something tangible, actual, and real, and this prospect of creation - of being a creator - is what I'm actively striving toward because I derive so much satisfaction and excitement from building great products.
